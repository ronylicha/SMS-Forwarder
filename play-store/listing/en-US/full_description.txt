SMS Forwarder automatically forwards your incoming SMS and RCS messages to a phone number of your choice. No account required. No server. Everything stays on your device.

Perfect for:
- Receiving work SMS on a second phone
- Monitoring a family member's SMS (with their consent)
- Never missing an important verification code
- Centralizing SMS from multiple phone lines

RELIABLE FORWARDING
- Triple message capture: SMS BroadcastReceiver + RCS ContentObserver + NotificationListener
- Smart deduplication to prevent duplicate forwards
- Long SMS support (multi-part messages over 160 characters)
- Automatic retry x3 with progressive delay on failure
- Manual resend from message history
- Anti-loop protection (automatic SIM number detection)

ADVANCED FILTERING
- Whitelist: forward only specific numbers or keywords
- Blacklist: block certain senders or content
- Combine both for precise control

MODERN INTERFACE
- Material You design with dynamic colors (Android 12+)
- Automatic dark mode (follows system theme)
- Full history with search and status filters
- Detailed statistics with daily chart
- Android widget to toggle forwarding with one tap

FLEXIBLE CONFIGURATION
- Multi-SIM support: choose which SIM card sends forwarded messages
- CSV export of complete history
- Test SMS to validate your setup
- Guided onboarding on first launch

MAXIMUM RELIABILITY
- Foreground Service with persistent notification
- Automatic restart after device reboot
- Runs in the background without interruption

PRIVACY FIRST
- Zero data collection, zero analytics, zero tracking
- No internet connection required
- 100% local storage on your device
- SMS transit via your carrier network only
- Open source code (AGPL v3 license)

PERMISSIONS USED
- SMS: to receive, read, and forward messages
- Phone: for multi-SIM detection and anti-loop protection
- Notifications: to capture RCS messages and display service status
